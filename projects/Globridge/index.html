Problem Statement

Each year thousands of refugees are forced to leave their homeland to escape political turmoil, natural disasters, war , and more often than not death. Leaving everything behind, these refugees move into new communities with little understanding of the resources made available to them. Our goal is to help these refugees and international students by utilizing existing learning resources and promote academic connections, sparking their intellectual growth in a foreign country.


Our Solution

Globridge is an online community supported by teachers and volunteers to help publish professional and user-generated resources while accommodating all languages, race, cultures, and backgrounds to unite it’s students into a common goal: to expand their learning. The goal of the site is to limit the amount of interactions to prevent users from being overloaded with information and overwhelmed by their technological ability. Globridge is designed to have low barriers of entry, so that even those without extended online experience should be able to navigate through the website.

Final Prototype

Home Page

This is the primary home page when visiting Globridge.

Create Account

This page gathers information from the user to customize the website content to be more relevant to them.

Register for Lectures

The user can type keywords, choose a filter, and navigate through recommended/hot/liked to find the courses they’re interested in and register for those courses.

Online Documents Page

Along with lecture courses, users can view an abundance of online materials through this document section.

Videos Page

This page provides video content for students that cannont attend in-person lectures or want to learn at their own pace.

Profile Page

This page gives the user some statistics to track their progress and feel that they are working towards their academic goals.
